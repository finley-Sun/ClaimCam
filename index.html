Proof of Concept

This application is a proof of concept built for the XRCC2026. The data visible throughout the interface like insured objects, claims, policies, weather and reconstructions are mocked due to the pricing and time constraints involved in integrating real APIs at this stage. The main goal of this demo is to showcase a compelling and realistic vision of how immersive technologies could transform the insurance claims experience in the near future, and to explore the technical feasibility of that vision with the tools and resources currently available to us. Despite our best efforts to make the experience as complete as possible, the lack of real API integrations means that some features, like the creation flow for objects and claims, are not fully functional.

The underlying technology is real and evolving.
